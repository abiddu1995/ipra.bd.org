Advancing Democracy Through Evidence-Based Policy

The Institute of Policy Research and Advocacy (IPRA) is an independent research organization dedicated to promoting human rights, democratic governance, and social justice through rigorous research and strategic advocacy.

Our Research Get Involved

About IPRA

Founded in 2024, we work to strengthen democratic governance through policy research, legal advocacy, and capacity building.

Our Mission

To empower civil society through evidence-based research and advocacy that promotes human rights, transparency, and accountable governance.

Our Vision

A world where policies are shaped by evidence, human rights are protected, and governments are accountable to their citizens.

Our Impact

Over 100 policy recommendations adopted since our founding, working in 15+ countries with 50+ partner organizations globally.
